History Quiz Instructions!


  1. First in order to start, you will have to insert your username and submit
  2. After pressing Start, the difficulty will pop up and you will be able to select the difficulty mode
  3. Score points for each correct answer, easy = 1, medium = 2 and hard = 5
  4. After selecting the difficulty mode, you will be taken to the quiz
  5. Where you will have four answer options for each question, Each mode has 10 questions
  6. Once the Answer is selected it will take you to the next question in 1.5 seconds
  7. The goal will be to get the highest score possible
  8. Have fun and enjoy the quiz :)
